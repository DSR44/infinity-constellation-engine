What You're Watching

A mathematical equation that compiles language into cosmic poetry and 3D star systems. No AI. No neural network. No cloud. Every output has never existed before and never will again.

Every 18 seconds a new phrase enters the equation. You can also type anything yourself. The same word typed twice will produce completely different results — different engine, different tokens, different universe.

How It Works

Your text + compilation count → SHA-256 hash → 3-bit binary flag selects 1 of 8 poetic engines → hash seeds pick tokens from 7 cosmic pools (celestial, biological, planetary, cosmic, stellar, frequency, quantum) → the engine compiles unique poetry.

The same hash generates star positions, nebula shapes, orbital paths, and color palettes. Every universe is as unique as the language it was born from.

It Evolves

The constellation ages with every compilation. That age shifts how tokens are selected, so the equation itself changes over time. High-vitality outputs trigger cosmic cycles that further shift the mathematics.

Your evolution persists across sessions. Close the tab, come back tomorrow — the constellation remembers how far it expanded. The same input today produces different output than it will next week.
